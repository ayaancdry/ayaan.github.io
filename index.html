ABOUT ME


TL;DR: Name’s Ayaan, creates cool stuff. Hire him.

Hey there! I’m Ayaan, a creative technologist and product designer based in Columbus, Ohio, and with roots in New Delhi, India. I design, code, edit, beatbox, and bike! I'm currently a sophomore and Schimmel Scholar at Ohio Wesleyan University, where I'm studying Computer Science and Psychology with a minor in Anthropology. Over the summer of 2023, I completed a Digital Product Management internship at Elevance Health. Previously, I’ve worked with App24 India as a Product Designer. At OWU, I'm a part of The OWtsiders A Capella group, and work at The Woltemade Center as a Design and Marketing strategist. I am also handling event logistics at Pinnacle, The Olympics of Hackathons . Apart from that, I'm heading Outreach and Design at techCircuit, a platform for connecting high school tech enthusiasts in India. I cofounded Cryptocracy, an international cryptic hunt and CTF for two years, where we saw global participation in thousands and coordinated with huge sponsors to give out prizes worth $7,000.

In the past, I have been actively involved with The Girl Code, a non-profit aimed at introducing tech and programming to young women worldwide, and Femme First Foundation, an organization passionate about bringing women into political leadership.

My school life was cool. In my time as the Vice President at CORE, the computers and technology society at my high school- Delhi Public School, Dwarka, I helped conduct tech symposiums with over 600 participants each year for 4 years, and as a part of a training camp, I actively mentored 500+ students in design, development, and video editing.
